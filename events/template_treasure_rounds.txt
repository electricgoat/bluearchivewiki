This minigame is similar to the Inventory Management seasons of [[Events/Mini_Event/SCHALE_Settlement_Task_with_General_Student_Council|SCHALE Settlement Task with General Student Council]] mini-event. Players must reveal hidden treasures on a {{rounds[0]['TreasureRoundSize'][0]}}x{{rounds[0]['TreasureRoundSize'][1]}}-tile board. 
Revealing any tile costs {{wiki_price}} and rewards {{wiki_cell_reward}}. Treasure rewards are obtained once all tiles of that treasure are revealed. Players can choose to advance to the next board after at least one treasure has been fully revealed; though Round 7+ rewards are substantially lower, so moving on before all/most of the board treasures are revealed is not advised.
